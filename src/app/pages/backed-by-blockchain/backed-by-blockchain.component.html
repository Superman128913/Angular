Our entire platform and all of our applications are backed by blockchain.

We view blockchain as the ultimate facilitator of a comprehensive compliance strategy with the ability to not only satisfy, but exceed, customer, regulatory, statutory, and internal requirements.

Blockchain enables trust between organizations who do not know each other.
Blockchain is a decentralized structure that enables real-time data sharing among manufacturers, suppliers, and distributors while reducing points of weakness.
Blockchain improves security and privacy by creating an unalterable record of transactions with end-to-end encryption, which reduces fraud and unauthorized activity.
Blockchain reduces costs by creating efficiencies that reduce manual tasks such as manipulating data by reporting and auditing.
Blockchain increases speed by eliminating intermediaries so transactions are handled faster than past methods.
Blockchain increases visibility and traceability by tracking the origins of an asset, both tangible (part, product, article, etc.) or intangible (document, drawing, contract, etc.), to confirm they're authentic instead of counterfeit.
Blockchain is immutable and ensures transactions can't be changed or deleted.
Blockchain gives an organization control of their data with the ability to decide what to share, with whom and for how long, with these attributes being enforced by smart contracts.
Blockchain converts the value of an asset into a digital token recorded and shared on the blockchain.
Blockchain provides a means of compliance for various, and increasingly difficult, regulatory, statutory, and customer requirements.
