Our mission is to simplify the requirements of aerospace and defense by providing next-generation technologies that advance the supply chain.

Our story

Aeroplicity is building the world's first blockchain backed aerospace and defense platform.

Times have changed, the requirements to do business have been raised for all aerospace and defense suppliers. This has left numerous smaller companies either unable to meet certain contractual requirements, unable to compete directly with their larger competitors, or, at the very least, raising their prices to cover their increased expenses. Starting a business or launching a product has also become more complex requiring a larger upfront investment of time, resources, and even knowledge before business can be conducted and revenue generated.

We believe there is a simpler way. A way in which small businesses can not only satisfy, but exceed, these requirements with less time, resources, and knowledge than if they were to build out a compliance strategy and externally procure or internally develop tools to help them meet their strategy. While there are numerous tools that may meet a single requirement, our platform aims for every one of our applications to be completely and seamlessly integrated with each other all while being backed by blockchain.

If you believe in our mission, join us in building it.
